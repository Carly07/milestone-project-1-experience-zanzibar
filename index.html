Zanzibar

One of the worlds great cultural crossroads, where Africa meets Arabia, Zanzibar is an archipelago of historic Indian Ocean islands awash with atmosphere, intrigue and idyllic beach experiences.

Highlights

Beautiful Beaches

Idyllic white powdered sands lapped by the warm turquoise waters of the Indian Ocean provide the perfect place to relax and soak up the sun.

Stunning Sights

Discover the island's rich cultural history as an important trade or spice centre

Awesome Activities

Whether it's diving, water sports or wildlife that takes your fancy, Zanzibar offers it all
